About Me

My name is Mark Hanny and I have designed and published dozens of tabletop board and card games. My goal is to produce remarkable games with imaginative themes that stimulate the strategic and creative mind.


Joe Magic Games has made and published dozens of games since its start in 1990. The company specializes is Euro-style games that lean more heavily on decision making and lightly on luck. Games typically have fantasy, medieval, or science fiction themes. Experienced hobby game players will find the games quick and easy to learn. Occasional game players may find the games more challenging. Most games take an hour or two to play and are for 3 to 5 or 6 players and are most enjoyed by game players 12 years and older
